Our non-profit mission began in the early days of April 2022 when our Graph Theory professor tasked us with creating a website supporting any cause and/or need. This website will not only provide the reader with great insight on several endangered species but also showcase our ability to efficiently communicate and work as a team to create a website, resolve issues, and make use of several github features.

Alternatively, we are four ‘hardworking’ students who deserve an A on this project and this website will become a starting point for the second portion which includes taking the DOM (Document Object Module) of our respective pages and creating a graph, adjacency list/matrix, and data structure with attributes.
